Guacamole Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 chopped avacados
  • 1 juiced lime
  • 2 chopped tomatoes
  • 1 T chopped cilantro

Directions

  • Mash the ingredients. Add salt, cumin, garlic, and cayenne to taste.
